About CSB Bank
Established in 1920, CSB Bank Ltd (Formerly The Catholic Syrian Bank Limited ) is one of the fastest-growing private banks in India with a significant branch presence in South India and a fast-growing network across India. The Bank has four principal business areas, namely, SME banking, retail banking, wholesale banking, and treasury operations.
In recent years the Banks accelerated it’s transformation process to become a new-age, profit-making bank with increased leadership, product development, digital banking technology and risk management capabilities. As the future road map is set to further build and scale its operations, with a strong governance structure the Bank is also increasing its focus on environment and social banking initiatives.
